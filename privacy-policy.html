Privacy Policy


Last Updated Date: [02-Sep-2024]

At Quick Cab Services, we value the privacy of our customers and are committed to protecting their personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use and protect it, and the rights you have regarding your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Identification Information: When you register an account on our App, we may collect personal information, including but not limited to: Name, Contact details (phone number, email address), Business details (cab service details, license information), KYC information (identity documents, vehicle registration, etc.).
We collect information about your interactions with the App, including: Enquiries and leads posted by you, Communication preferences, Device information (IP address, operating system, device type).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

To create and manage your account.
To enable communication between cab owners/drivers and vendors.
To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
To verify your identity and complete KYC processes.
To communicate with you about your account or transactions with us.
To send you updates, promotional materials, or notifications.

3. Data Protection and Security We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

Data Encryption: Securing sensitive information during transmission and storage.
Access Controls: Restricting access to personal data to authorized personnel only.
Regular Monitoring: Conducting regular security audits and updates to our systems.

4. Sharing Your Information We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except as described below:

Other users of the App (e.g., when you respond to an enquiry or lead).
Third-party service providers (for identity verification, payment processing, etc.)
Legal Requirements: If required by law, to comply with a legal obligation, or to protect our rights and safety.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access: To request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
Correction: To request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
Deletion: To request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain conditions.
Opt-Out: To opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us.
